Comment (by Anthony Chan):

 Do you have icc installed.  If you do, then configure mpich2 as

 mpich2-1.0.7/configure CC=icc CXX=icpc F77=ifort F90=ifort....

 If not, just remove CC and CXX from the above configure command.
 Send us the configure.log and config.log again if there is still problem.
