Hi,
  I am assuming that the default converter might not be rlog-->slog2 converter (it could be clog2-->slog2 converter).
  This is what I do,

1) Launch Jumpshot from app menu in "Program files"
2) Select "File-->Convert"
3) Select the converter at the top as "RLOG-->SLOG_2"
4) Select the "Input file..." (Your rlog file) & specify an "Output file"
5) Click on "Convert"
6) Click "OK" ---> Now Jumpshot opens the log file in slog2 format

 Let us know if it works for you.
