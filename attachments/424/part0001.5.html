 Hi,
  To use threads in your MPI program launch your MPI job with the multithreaded sock channel (mpiexec -n 2 -channel mt multithreaded_mpi_pgm.exe).
  The next beta release of MPICH2 (in couple of weeks) will include a new channel on windows called nemesis. You might want to try that out too (supports multithreaded MPI pgms & has better performance than sock channel).
